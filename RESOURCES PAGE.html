Textbooks

  • Introduction to Algorithms - Cormen, Leiserson, Rivest, Stein

    A comprehensive guide to algorithms, widely used as a standard textbook in computer science courses.

  • Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach - Russell, Norvig

    This book provides an in-depth exploration of artificial intelligence concepts and applications.

  • Database Systems: The Complete Book - Garcia-Molina, Ullman, Widom

    An authoritative resource on database systems, covering theory, design, and implementation.

Online Courses

  • Machine Learning - Coursera

    Learn machine learning from the co-founder of Coursera, Andrew Ng. This course covers a wide range of topics and practical applications.

  • Data Science Specialization - Johns Hopkins University

    Explore the fundamentals of data science and gain hands-on experience with real-world projects in this specialization.

Research Journals

  • Journal of Artificial Intelligence Research (JAIR)

    JAIR is an open-access journal covering all areas of AI research, including machine learning, robotics, and natural language processing.

  • ACM Transactions on Database Systems (TODS)

    TODS is a leading journal for research on database systems, featuring articles on theory, design, and applications.
